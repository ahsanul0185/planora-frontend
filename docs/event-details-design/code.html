About the Event

Join us for an immersive evening exploring the intersection of minimalist architecture and sensory silence. This curated event features leading designers and sound artists discussing how physical spaces influence cognitive clarity.

The session will include a keynote by Dr. Elara Vance on "The Psychology of Void," followed by a live ambient soundscape performance designed specifically for the gallery's acoustics. Attendees will experience firsthand how deliberate architectural choices can foster a profound sense of inner calm.
